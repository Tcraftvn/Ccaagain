The A350-1000 is a large widebody aircraft manufactured by Airbus. It comfortably accommodates from 350 to 410 passengers in a standard three-class configuration, and has a maximum fuel capacity of 156,000. The aircraft can fly up to 14,800km or 8,700nm at a maximum operating speed of Mach 0.89. The aircraft is powered by two Trent XWB-97 turbofan engines from Rolls-Royce
